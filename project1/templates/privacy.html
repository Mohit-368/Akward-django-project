01 Data We Collect

At Akward, we believe in data minimalism. We only collect what is absolutely necessary to keep the Vault running smoothly.

  • Account Data: If you register, we collect your email address and a chosen username.
  • Submission Data: The text, timestamps, and resonance metrics (likes/comments) of the awkward moments you post.
  • Technical Data: Basic log data such as IP address and browser type, kept strictly for security and anti-spam measures.

02 How We Use It

Your data is used to provide the Akward experience. We do not use your stories to train third-party AI models without explicit opt-in.

  • To display your posts in the community feed.
  • To moderate content and maintain the "Zero Toxicity" rule.
  • To securely verify your login credentials.

03 Data Sharing

Let‚Äôs be incredibly clear: We do not sell your personal data or your embarrassing stories to data brokers or advertising networks.

We only share data in the following highly restricted circumstances:

  • If required by a valid legal subpoena.
  • To protect the physical safety of our users or the public.

04 Cookies & Tracking

We use essential cookies purely to keep you logged in and to prevent cross-site request forgery (security). We do not deploy third-party tracking pixels to follow you around the internet.

05 Your Rights

You have total control over your digital footprint within our walls. Under GDPR and CCPA guidelines, you have the right to:

  • Request a full export of all data tied to your account.
  • Modify or delete any post instantly.
  • Permanently delete your entire account, scrubbing your email and posts from our active databases.

06 Contact the DPO

If you have questions about how we handle your data, or wish to exercise your rights, contact our Data Protection Officer directly.
